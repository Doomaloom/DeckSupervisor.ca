Splash 1

- Float on front and back (5 sec. each).

Splash 2

- Tread water 10-30 sec.
- Front Crawl and Back Crawl (5-10 m).
Splash 3

- Whip kick on back 10 m.

Splash 4

Splash 5

Splash 6

Splash 7

Splash 8

Splash 9

Little Splash 1

Little Splash 4

Little Splash 5
